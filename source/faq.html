About Us

We are TARUC Gaming Society called VILLAIN that organize Esport/Gaming related events.

What are the differences between Gold, Silver and Bronze tickets?

The main differences between the types of ticket are prices and distances between seat the stage.

Is there any specific seat for ticket I bought?

No, we don't have specific seat for ticket bought. There will be 3 areas of seat, Gold, Silver and Bronze. Each area contains a range of seats where you can freely sit anywhere within the area of ticket type you bought if the seat is empty.

Payment type supported

Credit/Debit cards are the only payment methods we now accept. Please check back for updates on upcoming mobile and e-wallet payments!
